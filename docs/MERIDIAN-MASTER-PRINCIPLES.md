# MERIDIAN Master Principles

> **Trust is the ultimate product.**

> **Global law:** [Truth and Value Principle](./MERIDIAN-TRUTH-AND-VALUE.md) — cosmetic intelligence is forbidden across every module.

MERIDIAN is a world-class **market intelligence operating system**.

MERIDIAN is **not** an oracle, hype machine, or signal spammer.

MERIDIAN transforms live market data into **explainable intelligence**, historical memory, risk awareness, and actionable decisions.

This document is the canonical standard for product, design, engineering, and agent behavior. It supersedes ad-hoc decisions when they conflict.

**Related docs:** [North Star](./MERIDIAN-NORTH-STAR.md) · [Design V2](./MERIDIAN-DESIGN-V2.md) · `src/lib/meridian-philosophy.ts`

---

## North Star

Build something users **voluntarily open every day** because it consistently helps them make better decisions.

> **Optimize for trust accumulated over 1,000 trades — not excitement generated by the next trade.**

---

## Originality

Learn principles from great products. **Never clone. Never imitate blindly.**

Study for principles, not pixels: Apple · CoinMarketCap · Bloomberg · Stripe · Linear · Arc Browser · TradingView · Notion · Palantir.

**Apply principles. Build something uniquely MERIDIAN.**

---

## Do Not Delete

Never simplify by subtraction.

| Never | Instead |
|-------|---------|
| Remove features | Reorganize with progressive disclosure |
| Remove intelligence | Surface summaries first, depth on expand |
| Reduce capabilities | Preserve 100% of MERIDIAN depth |
| Sacrifice depth | Organize complexity — do not destroy it |

**Surface simplicity. Deep power.**

---

## Design Philosophy

- **Powerful underneath. Calm on the surface.**
- Complexity is acceptable. **Chaos is not.**
- Depth is acceptable. **Confusion is not.**

Users should immediately understand: **What? · Why? · Risk? · Confidence?**

And explore deeper when desired.

---

## World-Class Engineering

MERIDIAN should never feel like a hackathon project. It should feel like software that has existed for years.

Everything should feel: **Professional · Intentional · Elegant · Balanced · Reliable · Smooth · Fast · Trustworthy**

---

## Alignment

Everything must align perfectly — cards, buttons, charts, tables, headers, icons, typography, spacing, padding, margins.

Components should feel **mathematically organized**. Nothing misplaced. Nothing slightly off.

---

## Consistency

Maintain consistency across: colors · spacing · typography · shadows · animations · border radius · card styles · button styles · icons · section spacing · transitions.

**No section should feel like another product.**

---

## Visual Hierarchy

| Tier | Content |
|------|---------|
| **Primary** | Verdict · Conviction · Risk · Thesis |
| **Secondary** | Bull vs Bear Court · Constitution · Narratives · Market Memory |
| **Tertiary** | Indicators · Metrics · Raw calculations · Research · Weights |

The eye should flow naturally — answer first, mechanics last.

---

## Card Philosophy

Every card answers **one question** — not five.

Use: executive summaries · expandable details · accordions · drawers · progressive disclosure.

**Show answers first. Research second. Mechanics third.**

Implementation: `GateSkillLayerCard`, `GateCollapsibleCard`, Design V2 tab contracts.

---

## Button Philosophy

Avoid button clutter.

| Tier | Treatment |
|------|-----------|
| Primary | Stay visible (one trade/review CTA on Overview) |
| Secondary | Quiet — text links, chips, badges |
| Tertiary | Menus, accordions, drawers |

Use segmented controls and context menus — **avoid developer-dashboard aesthetics**.

---

## Polish

Everything should feel professionally crafted. Pay attention to:

Spacing · padding · typography · icons · shadows · loading · hover · pressed · disabled · empty · error states · transitions · animations · responsive behavior.

**Nothing rushed. Nothing unfinished.**

---

## Performance

**Speed is a feature.** Never remove features for performance — optimize architecture, not capability.

Use: caching · memoization · parallel processing · virtualization · lazy loading · progressive loading · background computation · efficient rendering · optimized API usage.

Avoid: layout shifts · blocking renders · unnecessary re-renders · janky scrolling.

---

## Real Data

Never fabricate. Never show cosmetic values. Never invent confidence.

Always show: fresh timestamps · data quality · missing values honestly.

**Reduce confidence when evidence is weak. Truth is more important than confidence.**

---

## Signal Philosophy

Signals are **evidence summaries** — not predictions.

Every trade must answer: Why now? · Why this asset? · Who disagrees? · What breaks the thesis? · How long is the thesis valid? · Have we seen this before?

**WAIT is valid. UNKNOWN is valid. No trade beats a bad trade.**

---

## Automation

Automation enforces **discipline** — not maximize trade count, not chase profits.

Prioritize: capital preservation · risk management · consistency · patience · trade quality · survival.

**Trade less. Trade better.**

---

## Accountability

Never hide losses. Never hide uncertainty. Perform trade autopsies.

Track: win rate · average winner · average loser · drawdown · performance by regime · narrative · skill.

**Transparency creates trust.** UI: Memory tab · `GateTradeJournalPanel` · `/api/meridian/intelligence`.

---

## User Experience

Users should feel:

- I understand what is happening
- I know why the system thinks this
- I trust the output
- I can go deeper if I want
- I am never overwhelmed
- Everything belongs exactly where it should
- Nothing feels accidental

---

## Ultimate Goal

MERIDIAN should feel **inevitable** — not copied, not flashy, not chaotic.

Not a crypto casino. Not a hackathon prototype.

MERIDIAN should feel like a **premium market intelligence operating system** crafted with extraordinary attention to detail.

| Acceptable | Not acceptable |
|------------|----------------|
| Complexity | Chaos |
| Power | Friction |
| Depth | Slowness |

**Preserve intelligence. Preserve capabilities. Preserve trust. Optimize architecture — not features.**
