## MERIDIAN — short product summary (from this chat)

**North Star:** Trust is the product. Optimize decision quality over 1,000 trades — not excitement on the next trade.

### Core identity
- **MERIDIAN is an evidence engine, not an oracle.** Signals summarize evidence; they are not predictions.
- **Truth & Value law (global):** never fabricate data, confidence, or reasoning. Missing inputs must show **DATA UNAVAILABLE**.
- **Skills are deterministic calculators** (evidence only). They never output BUY/SELL.
- **LLM is narration only** (near the end), never the source of market numbers.

### Overview is sacred (Executive Desk)
Overview answers one question: **“What should I know and what should I do right now?”**

- **ONE TRUTH:** only the **position router verdict** (LONG / SHORT / FLAT) is the final decision on Overview.
- Everything else (constitution bias, momentum, skills, agents, chamber votes) is **explanation**, never a competing headline.
- **No conflicting hierarchy:** users should never see ENTER LONG / HOLD / DENY / FLAT at the same level.
- **Five‑second comprehension:** answer first, explain second, depth third. Calm on the surface.

### Signal generation philosophy (activity is not the goal)
- **FLAT is a position** (not failure). Professional systems spend a lot of time doing nothing.
- **WAIT / UNKNOWN are valid answers.** No trade beats a low‑quality trade.
- MERIDIAN must **never bias** toward LONG, SHORT, or FLAT — direction emerges from weighted evidence.

### Decision Engine (evidence synthesis)
- Nine layers: regime, trend, momentum, liquidity, RS, structure, narrative, memory, court.
- Scores: **Long · Short · Hold · Conflict · Uncertainty · Data quality** (not “7/9 = LONG”).
- **Directional neutrality** — no preferred direction; FLAT when disagreement dominates.
- **Router ONE TRUTH** on Overview; decision scores live on Technical tab / intelligence API.
- Multi-timeframe 3m–1w; higher TF anchors horizon. Risk/regime/liquidity can veto.

### Tab contracts (progressive disclosure)
- **Overview:** router verdict (ONE TRUTH), thesis, court, constitution summary, narrative, memory compact, primary action.
- **Technical & Reasoning:** layer votes, momentum/RSI/MACD/volatility/liquidity, agent pulse, consensus engine, provenance/audit.
- **Rules & Spec:** constitution articles, weights, sizing, thresholds, methodology.
- **Replay:** backtest timeline, evidence over time, accountability.

### Accountability
- Publish losses and drawdowns. Trade journal + autopsy are mandatory; never hide failures.
